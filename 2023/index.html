Welcome. To the only bar crawl for the people, by the people, of the people.

Back in 2019, we had an idea: Instead of standing in long lines, in the cold for over advertised, underwhelming bar crawls, why not create our own?

One that’s outdoors, on rooftops, at all our favorite places and gives people a reason to truly celebrate the start of summer. And thus JUNEFEST was born.

The inaugural JUNEFEST was a smashing success in 2019.

Check out some of our favorite moments below!

The Trek Map

A layout of our journey. Courthaus Social, Pinemoor, Pamplona, and the Lot. Many begin the trek, but few finish. Click on these locations on the map to check out their menus and plan your attack. Will you be among the few?
